About the Artist

Life

Hi, my name is Grafton Botelho I was born in Las Vegas, Nevada. Shortly after my parents moved us to Utah where I have lived ever since. I am curretnly studying graphic design at Utah Valley University and work at a cute print shop called Persnickety Prints. In my freetime I like to learn and grow as an artist. Focusing on family and friendship and all the relationships that come into my life allows for growth in multiple ways.

Why Art

Creating symbolic realism has been incredibly important to me because it allows me to express deeper emotions and ideas in a way that feels both personal and meaningful. By blending realistic imagery with symbolic elements, I can communicate complex themes that go beyond the surface, making my work more impactful. This style has been a useful resource in my life because it helps me process my own experiences, offering a way to reflect on emotions and moments that might otherwise be hard to articulate. It provides a unique lens through which I can explore and understand the world around me.
