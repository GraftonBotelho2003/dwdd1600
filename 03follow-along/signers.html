Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
The principal author of the Declaration and later the 3rd President of the United States.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence who became the 2nd President of the United States.
Benjamin Franklin
A well-known polymath, diplomat, and Founding Father, instrumental in securing French support during the Revolutionary War.
Samuel Adams
A leader in the American Revolution, known for organizing the Boston Tea Party.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famously known for his bold signature on the Declaration.

These men, along with the others who signed the Declaration, played a crucial role in the founding of the United States. Do you have a favorite among them?
